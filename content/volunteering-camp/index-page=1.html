Volunteering to the Camp

The DrupalCampCT organizers are seeking volunteers to help with the creation of our camp for both before the camp meets and during the day of the camp.

Volunteers are needed in advance of the event to assist with organizing the structure, logistics, program, promotion and fundraising needed to create a successful DrupalCampCT.

Volunteers are needed during the day of the event to assist with registration, setup, tear down etc.

Please check back to this page for opportunities to get involved and volunteer, or contact the DrupalCampCT organizers to get involved today.
