Design and Build Lean, Structured Content

Submitted by skowyra on Fri, 08/01/2014 - 10:10

In our massively multidevice world, we want to make sure our content travels well. Designing and structuring content and content types should be one of the first steps taken in the design process.

In this session we'll look at incorporating content strategy into the design process in order to build better content types including:

  • Overview of Content Strategy as it relates to discovering content types
  • Finding content patterns
  • Separating content from presentation and design

This session will use the Drupal Content Management Framework for examples, but the process can be applied to any system or design.

Note: This session will not cover Lean Content in the current marketing sense. For our purposes, lean means designing structured content free from presentation information.
